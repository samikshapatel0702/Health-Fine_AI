Welcome to HealthFine AI

HealthFine AI is a smart healthcare website that helps users explore common lifestyle diseases, understand symptoms, and get simple risk analysis with personalized wellness tips.

What this website does

  • Provides information about common lifestyle diseases
  • Lets users select symptoms and analyze possible risk
  • Shows personalized precautions and smart health tips
  • Includes a quick lifestyle risk quiz
  • Includes a BMI calculator for health awareness
  • Includes a water intake tracker

    About the Project

    HealthFine AI is a college project designed to spread awareness about lifestyle diseases and preventive healthcare through a simple, interactive dashboard.

    Project Objectives

    • Increase awareness about lifestyle diseases
    • Promote preventive healthcare habits
    • Provide a simple symptom checker
    • Encourage healthier daily choices
